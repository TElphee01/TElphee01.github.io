Web Development

For my CIS Capstone project during the Fall 2020, four other teammates and I worked as a virtual agile team to develop a web platform for JMU professors to host CyberDay! - A fun filled day on campus for middle school students to learn more about technology.
To meet our needs we used C#, ASP.Net, JavaScript, and Sql Server to develop a fluent platform, securely create various accounts, send emails, communicate with parents, and much more. Once finished our website was hosted on AWS Elastic Beanstalk.

During the summer of 2020 I used the short time between summer classes and my final fall semseter to build this website, TannerElphee.io. Starting with a common StartBootstrap theme, I've made this static website my own. TannerElphee.io is hosted for free on GitHub.com.
